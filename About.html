About Bay Max

What is Bay Max?

    Bay Max is a robot created by our team for the purpose of helping someone. Our team consists of Chloe, Diana, Elsa, Irie, Jazmine, and Samia who all met and worked together to build the robot through Girls Who Code. This robot was inspired by Bay Max from the movie Big Hero 6 where Bay Max is an inflatable robot who serves as a personal healthcare companion. Bay Max’s screen will provide a website with information and tools to assist the person using it. Bay Max’s main purpose is to inform others and help people dealing with mental and health problems. Bay Max offers many tools such as information about health, meal plans, healthy activites, and much more, which all come from one webiste that navigates Bay Max's activities.
